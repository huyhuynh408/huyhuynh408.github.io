A meticulous accountant recently turned web developer. I enjoy organization and making sure things are done properly. As a team member, I put the team before myself to achieve our goals. I thrive in environments where ego is set aside and the product being finished by the deadline is top priority.

Coding is where I find a lot of satisfaction in flexing new parts of my brain everyday.

I want to make meaningful things that are of use to the world, enrich the lives of others and that give back to the dev community. In the meantime, I will continue working on side projects I am passionate about until I find a job where I can shine as a developer and a person.
